Hi, I'm Sebastien Kempf

After over 7 years of managing supply chain, procurement and logistics international projects, I decided to take on a new challenge: learn different technical skills and create web and mobile applications.
So I enrolled in Le Reacteur coding bootcamp in Paris beginning of 2020 and started coding my first web and mobile applications in React and React Native. The creation process was super exciting!

My education and work experiences have taught me the need for constant learning and adapting which are benefiting my new career ; I am always amazed by the breadth of possibilities offered by new technologies.

I still very much like the operational aspects of moving projects forward. To that end, I advocate for Agility and as such, am a certified Scrum Master (PSM I) and Scrum Developer (PSD I).
